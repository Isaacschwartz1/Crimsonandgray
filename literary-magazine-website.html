About the Magazine

Literary Horizons is Saint Joseph's University's premier literary magazine, published biannually. Founded in 2002, the magazine showcases poetry, prose, and visual art from talented students, faculty, and alumni.

Our mission is to cultivate a vibrant literary community on campus and provide a platform for creative expression. Each issue is curated by our dedicated student editorial team and faculty advisors.
